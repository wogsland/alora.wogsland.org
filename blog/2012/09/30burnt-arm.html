30th September, 2012

Hey guess what, guys?
I'm in a whole bunch of pain right now. For those of you that don't know I have thick curly hair and tonight my friends decided to straighten it.  So Mimi and Madi got their straighteners out an went to work.  Well, you know how it is with friends everything goes everywhere so, they were taking a break from straightening my hair.  Lucky me, I leaned back to put my elbows on the ground and what did I find. A burning hot, water sizzling, super strength hair straightener.  I said ouch and then sort of went into shocked mode and asked for some ice.  Yeah, so now I'm sitting on my bed in the dark with ice and netflix ready to go. It's hard to type so I'm out.

Alora signing off.
