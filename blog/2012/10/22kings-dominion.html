Okay so on Saturday six of us went to King's Dominion an amusement park nearby. It was super fun.  Also it is super small. I walked all over the park at least three times.  We got there around noon and we left around ten. That's ten hours of amusement park fun and it was awesome.  I rode the Dominator, the Berserker, Boo Blasters on Boo Hill, the Volcano, Shockwave, and the Intimidator.  It was awesome! Also, I went into a scary corn maze and walked through "Scare zones" I ran around screaming and I rode a roller coaster standing up.  It was super fun.
