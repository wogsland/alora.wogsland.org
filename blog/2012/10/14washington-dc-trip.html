14th October, 2012

Okay so yesterday I went to Washington DC and had tons of fun.  The PEG group that went toured the National Museum of Women in the Arts which was pretty cool. There were lots of paintings and sculptures, but there was also a rock-and-roll exhibit where they had Lady Gaga's meat dress. Sadly they didn't allow pictures so I don't have a picture, but it was pretty cool. It doesn't even look like meat anymore.  So the Women in the Arts museum had this huge foyerish area where the building had once been a movie theater and it was really pretty.

This is the foyer place in the Women in the Arts museum
Before the tour Brittan and I had gone to the DC Zara store to buy stuff with our little sister's name on it. However for some odd reason nothing had the brand name plastered on the front, but I got some pictures.

After our shopping of sorts and the tour we went back to the national mall and explored the Natural History Museum and I seriously want to live there. We wanted to see other things so I only got to see part of it, but oh my god was it cool.  When we were looking at the dinosaurs I felt so small, and the moon rocks and asteroids were just totally awesome.

I didn't even see a quarter of it and we were in there for over an hour.  I bought astronaut ice cream in the gift shop and a cool poster and a key chain.
