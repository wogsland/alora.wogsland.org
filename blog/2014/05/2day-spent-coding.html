I also watched an enlightening video by the vlog brothers about internet neutrality.

Then I went on to sign lots of petitions and get involved on keeping the internet neutral. If you click to watch the video on Youtube, there are a lot of links in the description, but I will also include some easy ways to speak out here. Now I am by no means an expert on this, but from what I know of the situation, it is something that needs to be fixed. I believe that we should keep the internet a safe open space where everyone has access to everything. Because who knows? What if people start not only making us pay for faster internet, but also certain websites. Then companies driven by economic incentives could drive the information that we have access to on the web. I am not entirely sure if these "new rules" could enable that, but they certainly seem to pave the way for it.
