Volcanic Cold by: Alora Wogsland
Gravel and ash rain from the crater.
Poisonous gas clouds the summit from below.
Out of the face of the mountain, burning lava drips.
The surrounding rock is red and swollen.
Ocean water fails to placate the mountain’s orifice.
The mountain rumbles.
Lava rockets out.
“Gesundheit.”
